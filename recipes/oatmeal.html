Oatmeal Recipe

This oatmeal recipe is easy!

I make this most days of the week, and the recipe is on the container.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Rolled Oats
  • 1/2 Cup of Milk or Water
  • 40g of Raisins
  • 1 Tbsp of Maple Syrup or Honey
  • Nut Butter : Optional
  • Berries : Optional

Assembly

  1. Place oatmeal & milk in a bowl
  2. Heat in microwave for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add topping, mix, and enjoy!
