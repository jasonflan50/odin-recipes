Hamburger Recipe

This hamburger recipe is great!

I found this recipe after watching Joshua Weissman way too much on YouTube. He is known for making everything from scratch but here i'm just going to give a condensed version.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (85%)
  • Buns (Brioche or Potato Rolls)
  • Cheese (Cheddar, American or Muenster)
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Dill Pickles
  • Bacon (or Bacon Jam)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Mustard

Assembly

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet until it starts to smoke.
  2. Sprinkle both sides of burger with salt & pepper.
  3. Place the burger on the skillet and let cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Dollop 1 Tbsp of mustard on the side face up, then flip.
  5. Assemble burger toppings on bun.
  6. Add cheese to burger, cover for last minute
  7. Place the patty on the bun, cover, enjoy!
