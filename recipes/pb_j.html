PB & J Recipe

This pb & j recipe is classic!

This is every kids favorite sandwhich, yet it can be made in so many different ways!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread (White, Whole Wheat or Sprouted)
  • Nut Butter (Peanut, Almond or Sunflower)
  • Jelly (Strawberry, Grape or Blueberry

Assembly

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet to medium heat.
  2. Spread nut butter on one slice, jelly on the other.
  3. Close the sammy and place it on a buttered skilet.
  4. Let one side brown, then flip.
  5. When both sides are brown, remove from skillet and plate
  6. Important! Slice diagonal, or it won't taste right!
